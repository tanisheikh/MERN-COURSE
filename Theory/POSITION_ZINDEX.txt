5 type of positions:
1) Static OR Initial: Top-Left Corner of Its Direct Parent
2) Relative: Margin Occupied In Relation With Direct Parent.
3) Absolute: Margin Occupied In Relation With Grand Parent.
4) Fixed: Margin Occupied In Relation With Full Screen.

z-index: 1 (UPPER SIDE)
z-index: -1 (LOWER SIDE)
