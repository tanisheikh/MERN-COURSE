(1) SGML: 
    Standard Generalized Markup Language.

	(a) Start with single word than word itself tag between 1 to 10 characters combination.

		Categories:
		1) Typograpgy - Text | NEWS PAPER
		2) List | NEWS PAPER
		3) Table | NEWS PAPER
		4) Form | NEWS PAPER
		5) Link | NEWS PAPER
		6) Extra Markup | NO NEWS PAPER

	(b) Id more than 1 word combinely creates a phrase than first character of each creates a tag.
		Example: 
			(1) Unordered List : UL
			(2) List Item : LI
			(3) Ordered List : OL
			(4) Definition List : DL
			(5) Data Title : DT
			(6) Data Description : DD
		
TWO type of path in extra mark up category:
1) Folder Inside: 
[--Name Of A Folder--] / [--Name Of A File-]

2) Folder Outside:
../ [--Name Of A File-]

	(b) If two words combinely define a phrase than first character of each word creates a tag.

(2) In pure HTML no end tag only start tag.
	<p>Hello Akshay

	<p><b>Hello</b> Akshay</p>
	   - XHTML (XML + HTML)
